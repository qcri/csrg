Research

We work on technical, multidisciplinary aspects of cyber security and its real-world applications. We collaborate with our partners on research projects that support the thriving cyber security ecosystem in Qatar and abroad.

Blockchains

We are developing a full-stack blockchain security analytics platform called Toshi. The platform provides answers to high-level queries such as "which Twitter accounts made Bitcoin payments to Silk Road." It supports custom tags and full-text search of annotated blockchain data at the address level. Toshi is designed for researchers and law enforcement agencies. We used Toshi to study user anonymity, monitor underground markets, and investigate financial fraud. Try it out here.

Forensics

Our research in forensics spans several exciting application areas. We build advanced capabilities to determine the source and veracity of multimedia files. In addition, we are developing a new class of file carvers that can recover encoded and compressed files when the file data is only partially available. In collaboration with our partners, we also investigate new ways of mining system audit data obtained from enterprise hosts to perform more reliable root cause analysis and attack mitigation.

Tools

  • VideoPRNUExtractor: A suite of methods for attribution of videos to their sources

  • Jpeg-Carver: A data recovery tool that can carve out partial image fragments in storage volumes and network flows
